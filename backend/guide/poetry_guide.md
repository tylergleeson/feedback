# Guide to Poetry

## Core Principles
1. **Imagery First**: Use concrete, sensory details
2. **Show, Don't Tell**: Let emotions emerge through images
3. **Sound Matters**: Consider rhythm and word sounds
4. **Economy of Words**: Every word should earn its place

## Structure Guidelines
- Vary line lengths for rhythm
- Use stanza breaks for breathing room

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Cliches and overused phrases
- Excessive adjectives
- Forced rhymes

## Tone
- Be authentic
- Match tone to subject matter


## Additional Guidance (Proposed)
- Consider the emotional arc of the poem
- Balance abstract concepts with concrete anchors
- Let white space work as punctuation

## Feedback-Based Additions
- Use active verbs and dynamic imagery
- Ground abstract ideas in physical, sensory details
- Build energy through line breaks and punctuation
